TRUCLAIR

Welcome! My name is Kevin Aromin, also known as Truclair. I'm a story artist and animator developing my own furry IP since 2019.

During my time at UC Davis, I spent many years developing animated projects for Aggiemation Eggheads, and worked as a Graphics Assistant Director (AD) for UC Davis Picnic Day. Aside from illustration, I honed my leadership skills as a choreographer and video editor for H2O Dance Crew, where I developed quarterly showcases and led our team to 2nd place in the annual competition. I find myself at home developing my rich inner worlds and would love to apply the skills I've gathered in larger-than-life projects.

Thank you for your time!
