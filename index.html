KEVIN AROMIN

TRUCLAIR

Welcome! My name is Kevin Aromin, also known as Truclair. I'm a story artist and animator developing my own furry IP since 2019.

During my time at UC Davis, I spent many years developing animated projects. I worked as an animator for Aggiemation Eggheads, and an Assistant Graphics Assistant Director (AD) for UC Davis Picnic Day.

Aside from my graphics work, I honed my leadership skills as a choreographer and video editor for H2O Dance Crew, where I worked on setpieces and led our team to 2nd place in Davis Dance Revolution 2025.

I find myself at most at home developing rich inner worlds, and I would love to contribute the skills I've cultivated in larger-than-life projects.
